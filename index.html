What is this event about?

This full-day event will be the kick-off event to a monthly meetup held at the Universidad Catolica MBA building to cultivate the Santiago startup community. Local entrepreneurs from StartUp Chile will present about their experience starting their companies and provide Q&A sessions to community members. The aim is to expose audience members to hot industries and entrepreneurship skills they need to know about in order to launch successful startups.
